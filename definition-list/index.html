Definition List

Definition List of Swords:

Kokiri Sword
The very first sword link obtains and starts the game with, keepsake of his hometown
Razor Sword
An upraded version of the Kokiri Sword, albeit temporary, loses its edge after 100 swings
Gilded Sword
An upgraded version of the Razor Sword, and does not lose its edge
Great Fairy Sword
A standalone sword requiring 2 hands, much stronger than the other ones. Given to Link by the Great Fairy
